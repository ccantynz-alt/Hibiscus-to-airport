### Email Enhancements:
1. **Premium Branding** - Elegant header with gold/charcoal theme
2. **Professional Language** - "Investment" instead of "Price", "Transfer" instead of "Booking"
3. **Service Expectations** - Clear list of premium features included
4. **Visual Hierarchy** - Better section organization with icons
5. **Correct Contact Info** - hibiscustoairport.co.nz branding
6. **Mobile Responsive** - Optimized for all devices
7. **Professional Tone** - Language befitting a premium service

### SMS Improvements:
1. **Concise Premium Format** - Essential info only
2. **Visual Elements** - Appropriate emojis for quick scanning
3. **Shortened URL** - Easy tracking link
4. **Professional Tone** - Elevated language
5. **Emergency Contact** - Clear support number

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary:** Charcoal (#1F2937) and Gold (#F59E0B)
- **Accent:** White and subtle grays

- **Professional:** Clean typography and spacing
